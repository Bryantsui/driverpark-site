How It Works

Four steps, no friction.

The current flow is intentionally simple — concierge-based, no tech integration needed.

Find & Park

Driver sees partner malls in-app and parks at the nearest one.

Make a Purchase

Any merchant, any amount. Typical basket is HK$70–120.

Show Card at Concierge

Receipt + digital membership card shown; 1-hour parking validated on the spot.

Check-In Logged

Concierge check-in is recorded. No QR scan or receipt upload required.
